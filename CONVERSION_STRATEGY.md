## üí∞ Pricing Psychology Breakdown

**Research Shows**:
- 2 options = analysis paralysis
- 3 options = "Goldilocks sweet spot"
- 4+ options = decision overload

### Why Pro is Featured?

**The "Decoy Effect"** from behavioral economics:

```

Starter: ‚Çπ999   (Too cheap, seems low quality)

‚Üí Conversion rate: 2-3x HIGHER
```
